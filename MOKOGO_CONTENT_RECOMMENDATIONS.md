The home page has been successfully transformed from Gigly (financial platform) to MokoGo (room/roommate finding platform). Here are recommendations for presenting the content effectively.

### 2. Business Model Transformation
- ✅ Changed from financial services (drivers, investors) to room finding (Seekers & Listers)

### 3. Color Theme

## Content Presentation Recommendations

### Hero Section
**Current:** "Find Your Perfect Room & Roommate"
**Recommendation:** 
- Keep the current messaging - it's clear and direct
- Consider adding a sub-headline: "Verified listings. Direct contact. Zero brokerage."
- The CTA "Find Your Place" is perfect - consider adding a secondary CTA "List Your Space" for Listers

### About Us Section
**Current:** "For Room Seekers - Tired of searching for the Perfect Roommate?"
**Recommendation:**
- Split into two columns: "For Seekers" and "For Listers"
- Add visual distinction between the two user types
- Include quick stats: "2,500+ Listings" | "10,000+ Users" | "100+ Cities"

### Services Section
**Current:** 6 service cards
**Recommendation:**
- Reorganize into 2 main categories:
  1. **For Seekers:** Find Room, Verified Profiles, Smart Matching
  2. **For Listers:** List Your Space, Zero Brokerage, Safety First
- Make it visually clear which services are for which user type

### Why MokoGo Section
**Current:** 6 feature cards
**Recommendation:**
- Prioritize top 3-4 features:
  1. **Verified & Safe** (most important - trust factor)
  2. **Zero Brokerage** (key differentiator)
  3. **Smart Matching** (unique value)
  4. **Wide Coverage** (scale indicator)
- Consider adding trust badges: "ID Verified", "Property Verified", "Community Rated"

### How It Works
**Current:** 3 steps
**Recommendation:**
- Add visual step indicators
- Include screenshots/mockups of the platform
- Add separate flows for Seekers vs Listers if possible

### Testimonials
**Current:** Generic testimonials
**Recommendation:**
- Update with real Mokogo-specific testimonials
- Include both Seeker and Lister testimonials
- Add location tags (e.g., "Mumbai, India")
- Include photos of actual users (with permission)

### FAQs
**Current:** Generic questions
**Recommendation:**
- Add Seeker-specific FAQs:
  - "How do I verify my profile?"
  - "Can I contact owners directly?"
  - "What if I don't like my roommate?"
- Add Lister-specific FAQs:
  - "How do I list my room?"
  - "What information should I include?"
  - "How do I screen potential roommates?"

### Stats Section
**Current:** Generic numbers
**Recommendation:**
- Use real Mokogo metrics:
  - "2,500+ Live Listings"
  - "10,000+ Happy Users"
  - "100+ Cities"
  - "4.8★ Average Rating"
- Add growth indicators: "Growing by 200+ listings/month"

### 2. Social Proof

### 3. Call-to-Actions
- Primary CTA: "Find Your Place" (for Seekers)

### 4. Search Functionality
- Add prominent search bar in hero section

### 5. Content Sections to Add

- **Safety & Security:** Dedicated section explaining verification process
